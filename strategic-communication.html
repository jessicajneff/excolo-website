Workshop 01

Strategic Communication Planning

For organizational teams ready to move from strategy to action.

Overview

From strategy to action — together.

As organizational leaders, we know where we want to go and we can see what it takes to get there — but we can't get there alone. Execution depends on people, and people depend on communication.

This workshop is designed for organizational teams who are ready to move from strategy to action. We bring together a diverse team to work through a proven strategic communication process focused on a specific strategic challenge or initiative your organization is facing.

You leave with more than a document — your team will gain a shared framework, a common language, and the foundation of a strategic communication plan.

We think about strategic communication as three things: a process, a plan, and ultimately a capability. By working through all three together, your team doesn't just produce a deliverable — strategic communication becomes embedded in the organizational mindset.

What to expect

  • An initial intake conversation with your team lead to understand your goals and customize the workshop accordingly
  • A facilitated process that draws on diverse perspectives from across your organization
  • A working session format — participants engage, debate, and collaborate
  • A strategic communication plan foundation your team can act on immediately
  • Delivery in person (typically two full days) or virtually via Zoom (three shorter days)

Communication & Leadership Inventory

A personalized development program for leaders who want to lead with more clarity, confidence, and impact.

Overview

Tailored to where you are and where you want to go.

The Communication & Leadership Inventory starts with a one-on-one intake conversation to get a sense of where you are and where you want to go. From there, you'll receive a tailored recommendation from our targeted learning modules designed to strengthen the specific communication and leadership skills that matter most to you.

You'll work through material, complete exercises, and meet with a coach to debrief each topic.

How it works

1
Intake conversation — A one-on-one conversation to understand where you are and where you want to go.
2
Strengths evaluation — A tailored mix of assessments to help you take stock of your strengths and growth areas.
3
Learning modules — Work through targeted material and exercises at your own pace.
4
Coaching debriefs — Meet with a coach to debrief each topic and apply insights to your specific context.
Step 2

Communication & Leadership Strengths Evaluation

The best way to get where you want to go is to know what you bring to the table, what the impact is, and where you need to grow. We use a variety of inventories depending on your goals. These might include:

EQ-i

Emotional Intelligence

The Emotional Quotient Inventory is the gold standard emotional intelligence inventory. Based on a research-backed model, this inventory provides insight into social-emotional strengths and growth areas, with a focus on how emotional intelligence can be grown and leveraged for leadership success.

TKI

Conflict Style

The Thomas-Kilmann Conflict Mode Instrument is the most widely used conflict instrument. As the world changes faster and more dramatically, our need to navigate differences continues to accelerate. This inventory offers a window into your default styles and provides a framework and language to help align your style to the situation.

MBTI

Personality

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator is the world's most widely used personality assessment, offering insight into how you perceive the world, make decisions, and interact with others.

CliftonStrengths

Strengths

The CliftonStrengths assessment identifies your top natural talents, helping you understand where you have the greatest potential to develop strength and lead with confidence.

Step 3

Learning Modules

Based on your intake conversation and assessment results, we'll recommend a tailored selection of modules from the following areas:

Conflict Management & Difficult Conversations

Learn foundational principles and concrete practices for managing conflict and navigating difficult conversations.

Giving & Receiving Feedback

Learn to provide performance feedback that can be received constructively and drives real growth.

Motivation & Engagement

Gain concrete strategies for increasing employee motivation and engagement.

Strategic Communication

Gain the tools to implement a strategic approach to interpersonal and organizational communication.

Leveraging Communication Networks

Learn how to leverage formal and informal communication networks in your organization to your strategic advantage.

Presentation Skills

Delivery matters as much as content. Learn to present your ideas with confidence, clarity, and the kind of presence that makes your message land.

Managing Change

Whether you're navigating new processes, systems, technologies, or strategy, this module gives you the communication tools to lead your organization through transition effectively.

Leadership Context

Effective leadership requires understanding the unique design, culture, and constraints of your organization. Learn to read your environment and lead with greater impact within it.

Managing Diverse Teams

Build the skills to bring out the best in a diverse group of individuals, leveraging different perspectives and strengths for stronger team performance.
